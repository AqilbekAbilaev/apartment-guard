Definition:
This is telegram bot to automate the stuff which repeat all the time in the apartment. This project helps to keep transparency for everybody in the apartment and keep the apartment in order they agreee on
at the start. Bot enables duty tracking for different things like dish washing, throwing the trashes away, buying groceries and other stuff which can be configured later. This project also helps people in
the apartment to track their finance which spent on the common things(apartment budget).

Bot constantly notifies about common stuff like throwing trashes away and making dishes. Bot should automatically fetch all the members from the group. If someone wrote about that they did something, it is necessarily requires from other members to confirm. This helps to reduce cheating cases in the apartment among members. In this case person who is on duty cannot confirm.
